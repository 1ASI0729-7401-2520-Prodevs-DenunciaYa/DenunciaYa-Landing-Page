How does it work?

Our platform works in three simple steps to ensure transparency and speed:

Citizens

Select the category and describe the incident in detail.

Authorities

Receive the report in real time and assign responsible parties.

Follow-up

The citizen receives notifications about the status of the report.

About Us

DenunciaYa is a citizen initiative that seeks to strengthen the relationship between the population and municipal authorities. Our goal is to offer a reliable tool to register reports in an accessible and transparent way.

We constantly work to improve the user experience so that every report has a real impact on the community.
